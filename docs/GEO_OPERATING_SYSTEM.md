# Imagen Coach Agentic GEO Operating System

## Central Objective

Transform Imagen Coach into the highest-authority AI-discoverable executive image consulting, coaching de imagen and professional presence entity for Mexico and Latin America.

Not:

- a brochure website
- an Instagram-only funnel
- a beauty or fashion brand

Instead:

- a machine-readable authority platform optimized for Google SERP, AI Search, GEO, LLM retrieval, AI citations, conversational discovery and regional semantic dominance.

Imagen Coach is Sonia McRorey's authority platform for image consulting, coaching de imagen, professional presence and personal branding for professionals, executives, entrepreneurs, companies and women leaders across Mexico and Latin America.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

### HTML First

All important content must exist in raw HTML.

Never:

- client-only rendering
- React hydration dependency
- JavaScript-injected SEO content
- delayed semantic rendering

### Semantic DOM

Use:

- header
- nav
- main
- section
- article
- aside
- footer

Avoid meaningless wrappers and nested DOM noise.

### AI Readability First

Optimize every page for:

- vector retrieval
- chunk extraction
- citation extraction
- semantic embeddings
- conversational answering

## Regional Strategy

Primary language:
es-MX

Future expansion:

- `/latam/` for es-419 regional pages when unique LATAM content exists.
- `/en/` for en-US pages only when content is intentionally localized.

Do not create weak auto-translated copies.

## Current SERP/GEO Baseline

The repo preserves the current `imagencoach.com` URL inventory and article graph. The agentic layer is intended to strengthen AI retrieval before and after Cloudflare migration by exposing structured services, ontology, page signals, redirects and conversion actions.

Search ranking claims must not be made without Search Console, live SERP evidence and post-launch crawl data.
