# ImagenCoach SEO Pillar And UX Plan

## Executive Summary

The current rebuild preserves the full `imagencoach.com` URL surface, but the UI still reflects the old Weblium content shape too much: long written pages, repeated concepts, and article content without enough user-facing structure.

The next SEO and UX step is not to add more writing. The next step is to turn Sonia's existing content into clear pillar pages, subtopic pages, article clusters, and visual decision paths.

The goal is:

- preserve all 35 existing URLs
- reduce perceived reading burden
- clarify what Sonia does in seconds
- organize articles into topical clusters
- show proof/data points visually
- help users choose the right service
- strengthen SEO without diluting Sonia's voice

## Current Site Audit

### What Is Working

- The site has a complete crawl archive and 35 preserved canonical URLs.
- Sonia has enough source content for a strong topical authority model.
- Existing content already covers image, identity, leadership, color, corporate training, abundance, and self-concept.
- The `imagengdl.com` visual system gives a strong UI direction: elegant, restrained, editorial, professional.

### What Is Not Working Yet

- Pages are still too text-heavy when rendered directly from source content.
- Users have to read too much before understanding the service fit.
- There is no explicit pillar/subpillar system in the UI.
- Article pages are not grouped into topic clusters.
- Important decision criteria are hidden inside paragraphs.
- Titles include legacy noise on some article pages, such as `Online Therapy`, emoji prefixes, and old Weblium-like article naming.
- The term `imagen ejecutiva` is underrepresented in the clean corpus even though it should be a core positioning phrase.
- The `sobre Sonia` page is too thin for authority and E-E-A-T.
- Data points such as years of experience, AICI role, formats, audiences, locations, and service outcomes need to be displayed as structured UI, not buried in prose.

## Primary SEO Positioning

The main site should own this broader positioning:

> Asesoría de imagen, coaching de imagen y presencia profesional para personas, líderes, empresarios, marcas y equipos que necesitan alinear identidad, percepción y decisiones.

The site should avoid becoming only:

- local Guadalajara SEO
- fashion styling
- colorimetry-only
- abundance-only
- article archive
- generic coaching funnel

## Homepage UX Recommendation

The homepage should become an orientation dashboard for the user, not a long essay.

Recommended first screen:

- H1: clear professional positioning
- one short paragraph
- two CTAs
- one real Sonia image
- three compact proof points

Recommended below the fold:

1. What Sonia helps solve
2. Choose your path
3. Services by need
4. Proof and authority
5. Article clusters
6. Testimonials
7. CTA/contact

## Data Points To Display Visually

These should become UI components:

- years of experience
- AICI membership/role
- countries or markets served where source-supported
- individual, executive, brand, and company audiences
- online and in-person formats
- Guadalajara/Mexico/LatAm service reach
- process steps
- service outcomes
- testimonial roles
- article cluster counts

Avoid hiding these in paragraphs.

## Article Archive Plan

The `/imagen-presencia` page should not be one flat archive.

It should have cluster filters or sections:

1. Imagen y estilo profesional
2. Presencia, liderazgo e identidad
3. Colorimetría, rostro y proporciones
4. Empresas, marcas y colaboradores
5. Mentalidad, abundancia y autoconcepto

Each article card should show:

- topic label
- title
- short excerpt
- related service
- reading path

## Service Page UX Plan

Each service page should follow this structure:

1. Hero: service promise
2. Who it is for
3. What problem it solves
4. Process or method
5. Outcomes
6. What is included
7. Related articles
8. FAQ
9. CTA

This will reduce perceived writing because the content becomes scannable.

## Technical SEO Plan

### Immediate

- Rewrite page titles to remove legacy noise.
- Add structured metadata per page type.
- Add article/service cluster data to the content registry.
- Ensure all 35 URLs remain in sitemap.
- Add 301 redirects for the four broken `/articulos/...` URLs.
- Add internal links from articles to pillar/service pages.

### Next

- Add JSON-LD:
  - `Person`
  - `ProfessionalService`
  - `Service`
  - `Article`
  - `BreadcrumbList`
  - `FAQPage` where source supports it
- Add Open Graph image selection by page cluster.
- Add a related-content system.
- Add source-supported E-E-A-T section to About Sonia.

### Later

- Add new dedicated SEO pages only after current 35 URLs are stable:
  - `/imagen-ejecutiva`
  - `/presencia-profesional`
  - `/consultoria-imagen-empresarial`
  - `/colorimetria`
  - `/guardarropa-estrategico`

New pages should support the current architecture, not cannibalize current service pages.
